App Review Notes – Diabeaters

Purpose
• Lifestyle companion for people living with Type 1 diabetes (organisation & preparedness)
• Not a medical device; no medical advice or dosing guidance
• Features: supply tracking, routines, meal/exercise/travel planning, dashboard

Data sources
• Educational content is general, non-diagnostic, and cited in-app where applicable
• No real-time medical measurements interpreted
• No diagnosis, treatment, or cure claims

Safety (Guideline 1.4.1)
• Medical disclaimers shown during onboarding and in Settings → About
• Canonical disclaimer: "Diabeaters provides general lifestyle organisation for people living with Type 1 diabetes. It does not provide medical advice and is not a medical device. Always follow guidance from your healthcare professional."
• Native disclaimer string in ios/App/App/Disclaimer.swift

Account
• Email/password and OAuth (Apple, Google, Microsoft) via Supabase Auth
• Test account can be created by reviewer directly (no demo credentials required)

Network
• All content served over HTTPS only (NSAppTransportSecurity/ATS enforces HTTPS-only)
• External links open in SFSafariViewController (via @capacitor/browser when wired)

Privacy
• Minimal data; no protected health information required
• See docs/data_collection.md for details

Contact
• Support email/URL in Settings and on the Support URL
• Privacy policy URL and marketing URL in App Store metadata
